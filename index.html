About!

I was born and raised in Iran. I am currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey, under the supervision of Prof. Erdal Arıkan at the Telecommunication lab.

Prior to this, I completed my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees at Amirkabir University and Isfahan University, respectively, both in Pure Mathematics.

Currenly I am working on Polarization-Adjusted Convolutional (PAC) codes and my primary areas of interest in research include information theory, error correction coding, machine learning, deep learning, as well as analysis of algorithms.

You can contact me through email if you are interested in my work, want to collaborate, or just want to say hello!
